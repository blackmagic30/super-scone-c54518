Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 3rd April 2026

Split Seconds ("the App") is operated by Isaac De Worsop ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Split Seconds is designed to collect minimal data.

Usage Data

  • App launches
  • Gameplay events
  • Reaction time scores
  • Session duration

This data is used to improve gameplay performance and app stability.

Diagnostics Data

  • Crash logs
  • Performance data
  • Error reports

This information helps us identify and fix technical issues.

2. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks.

These ad providers may collect and process certain data to display relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers - IDFA)
  • Advertising data (such as ad interactions, impressions, and clicks)
  • Device type and operating system
  • General location (non-precise)
  • App interaction data

We do not directly collect or control this data. It is handled by the advertising providers according to their own privacy policies.

Where required by law, we will request your permission before enabling tracking for personalized advertising.

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS devices, you will be asked for permission to allow tracking across apps and websites.

If you allow tracking, third-party partners may use your data to deliver personalised advertisements across apps and websites.

If you decline tracking:

  • You will still see ads
  • Ads may be less relevant
  • No cross-app tracking will occur

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address (unless you contact us directly)
  • Physical address
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or camera data
  • Microphone data
  • Health or body data
  • Payment information

The App does not require user account registration.

5. Game Center

If you use Apple Game Center:

  • Your Game Center nickname and scores may be displayed publicly
  • Data is managed by Apple and subject to their Privacy Policy

We do not control Apple's handling of Game Center data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Apple Game Center
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google AdMob or similar)

These services may collect anonymous device-level information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App performance data

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Usage

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Analyze general app usage trends
  • Serve advertisements

We do not sell personal data.

We do not link collected data to your identity.

8. Data Retention

Diagnostic and usage data may be retained only as long as necessary to improve app performance and stability.

9. Children's Privacy

Split Seconds does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or serve personalised ads to children.

If you believe personal data has been provided by a child, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

11. Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect information from unauthorised access or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

12. International Users

If you access the App from outside Australia, your information may be processed in accordance with applicable laws in your region.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date.
