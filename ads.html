Advertising & Monetisation

Last Updated: 3rd April 2026

Split Seconds ("the App") includes optional advertising features to support ongoing development.

1. Rewarded Ads

The App currently uses rewarded advertisements only.

  • Ads are completely optional
  • Users may choose to watch an ad in exchange for in-game rewards (such as coins)
  • Rewards are granted only after the ad is successfully completed

The App does not require users to watch ads to continue gameplay.

2. Advertising Provider

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements.

AdMob Publisher ID: pub-3824021042171125

AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide ads, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • IP address (used for approximate location)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Apple's Identifier for Advertisers - IDFA, if permission is granted)
  • Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS devices, you may be asked to allow tracking across apps and websites.

  • If you allow tracking, ads may be more relevant
  • If you decline tracking, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized

Your choice does not affect gameplay functionality.

4. Personalised vs Non-Personalised Ads

The App may display:

  • Personalised ads (if tracking permission is granted)
  • Non-personalised ads (if tracking permission is denied)

5. app-ads.txt Authorization

This app authorises Google to sell advertising inventory on our behalf.

The authorised seller is listed in our app-ads.txt file:

google.com, pub-3824021042171125, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

6. No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly serve personalised ads to children.

This page may be updated as advertising features evolve.
